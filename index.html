03 — Projects

What I've Built

PROJECT / 001
Smart Car — Obstacle Detection System

Designed and assembled a sensor-driven autonomous vehicle prototype as part of a team workshop. The car uses ultrasonic distance sensors to detect nearby objects and automatically responds to avoid collision — simulating real-world proximity alert systems used in automotive and industrial robotics.

Worked on sensor integration, Arduino programming logic, and motor control. This project gave me hands-on understanding of embedded systems, real-time signal processing, and hardware-software co-design.
